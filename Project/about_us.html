About us

Code Web Development was founded in 2014. Our small team of developers strive to do the absolute best for our clients needs. We specialize in helping small businesses online, but have a large range of skills that allow us to meet almost any brief.

Our Team

Our small team has over 30 years combined experience in the industry. They come from a wide range of backgrounds, ranging from designers, developers to managers. You will be able to work closely with the team in order to receive a product best suited for your needs.

Where it all Began

Code began when two close friends decided to take something the loved as a hobby and turn it into a career. Since then they have been working toward creating the best web content they can, as well as gathering a strong team to expand the business.
