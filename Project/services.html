Services

At Code there is a wide range of services that our team are able to help you with. Over the years our team have worked on thousands of different projects for many clients with their own needs. This gives them the experience to fill your requirements. Some of the main things they can help with are:

Web Development

If you need a simple website for your business or project then we are able to help. Our team consists of designers, developers, managers and everyone else it takes in order to create what is right for your needs. Our team will help you through every step of the process, from initial planning to running and maintaining the website.

During the design and development phase the team will work hard on both usability and accessibility. These are important factors and can help you website reach more people than it would be able to otherwise.

Mobile & Tablet

With the increased popularity of smart phones people can have access to the internet within seconds at any time during the day. This can be a huge opportunity for your business as these people could be potential clients. Here at Code our team is experienced with the languages and frameworks needed to create applications for these devices. This can provide more features or services then a normal web page would be able to.

As well as applications, if needed we are also able to optimize your new or existing website for viewing on mobiles devices, allowing your website to be seen by more people in more places than ever before.

Contact us for more information

Web Applications

If a simple static website is simply not enough for your needs then we are able to help and create full web applications. These can be far more interactive and can provide a much more enjoyable experience for the user. Our team have worked on many different apps, from online stores to things like online content editing or video streaming.

You will be able to have a large input on these kinds of projects if you need in order to get a result that works best for you or your business. Having something like this can really make you stand out online.
